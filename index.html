THE RIPPLE EFFECT

How American Tariffs are Reshaping U.S. Commercial Building

Tariffs, once a simple tax, are now powerful policy tools. While intended to protect domestic industries, they create a cascade of financial and operational challenges for the U.S. commercial building sector, a vital engine of economic growth. This infographic explores the direct costs, supply chain disruptions, and strategic shifts defining the industry's response to a volatile trade landscape.
